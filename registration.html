How to Register

Follow these simple steps to join StateHela Agencies:

  1. Click on the Register button in the navigation bar.
  2. Fill out the registration form with your details.
  3. Enter a unique username eg. Grace254, James56, Sarah001, IvyKE
  4. Submit your details
  5. Pay the activation fee using the provided payment methods.
  6. Once payment is confirmed, your account will be activated.
  7. Log in to your account and start earning!
